About Soloist Projects

Soloist Projects (Pty) Ltd is a trusted plumbing and civil contractor based in Mitchells Plain, serving residential, commercial and infrastructure clients across Cape Town and the Western Cape.

Our skilled team provides professional plumbing installations, bulk water reticulation, civil pipeline construction and maintenance services. We deliver efficient solutions using modern materials and compliant installation standards.

With over 40 years of combined experience in plumbing, civil engineering and water infrastructure, we ensure reliable workmanship, safety compliance and long-lasting systems for every project.
